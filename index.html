About Me

My name is Brian Allen. I live in Salt Lake City, Utah with my wife and many pets. I have always loved the creative side of things. I have always enjoyed being able to creatively express myself through various projects, from writing leadership programs for youth at Rocky Point Haunted House, writing and directing interactive murder mysteries at the Castle of Chaos, and creating and running multiple tabletop gaming groups through epic adventures in Dungeons & Dragons. I really enjoy being able to tell stories, and see the outcome and impact I can have on others.

I live in Salt Laky City, Utah, with my lovely wife and a number of pets. I love the variety of unique biomes here in Utah, and getting to explore places unlike any other.

I love to play all types of games, from board and card games, to puzzles to roleplaying games. I love technology and video games, and I love to create. I can't wait to help you with your next project!
